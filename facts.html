Facts About the Royal Bengal Tiger

  • The Royal Bengal Tiger is the national animal of both India and Bangladesh.
  • The Bengal Tiger serves as a vital role of ancient Indian mythology, appearing on the coins of the South Indian dynasties Cholas, Cheras and the Pandyas. It also appears on the Indus-Valley era divine seal of Shri Pashupatinath, none other than the incarnation of Lord Shiva himself.
